OUR SERVICES

What do we have to offer?

Professional hitching company offering expert hitch installation, repairs, and maintenance. Specializing in trailer wiring and electrical services, we also provide a wide range of towing accessories for safe and efficient towing solutions.

Installation and Setup

Professional installation and setup of hitches on various vehicles, including cars, trucks, RVs, and trailers. Customers rely on the expertise of the hitching company to ensure proper installation for safe towing.

Repair and Maintenance

Maintenance and repair services are crucial to ensure the longevity and functionality of hitches. This includes inspections, repairs, and routine maintenance tasks such as lubrication and tightening of components.

Wiring and Electrical

Many towing setups require proper wiring and electrical connections between vehicles and trailers. This service involves the installation, repair, and maintenance of electrical systems, including trailer lights, brake controllers, and wiring harnesses.

Accessories

Providing a variety of essential towing accessories like ball mounts, couplers, safety chains, and more, ensuring customers have all necessary equipment for safe and efficient towing.
